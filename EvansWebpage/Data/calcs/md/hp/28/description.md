The HP 28C is HP's very first graphing calculator. Rather uniquely
amongst its kind, it adpots a clamshell design in order to fit the
full ABC keyboard on the left. It was also the first
widely-avaliable calculator to feature the capability to
manipulate symbolic algebraic expressions (a capablility we call
'CAS', or the Computer Algebra System, today). This, combined with
the powerful RPL language and input, marks it as the first in a
long line of greats.
