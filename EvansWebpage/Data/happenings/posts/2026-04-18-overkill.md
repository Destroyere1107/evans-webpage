Do you notice anything different on this site?

Of course you don't. No one visits this shit enough to.

Hopefully not. In case you didn't, I've transformed the project (with a teensy bit of help from Gemini) to run on ASP.NET!

Now, I can hear you. "Why? Why would you do that? What purpose would it serve?"

I got no damn idea\*. But I did it.

\*Actually, I do. (There used to be a dropdown here that you would click, but my modifications make that impossible now...)

Although much of the reason for doing this is 'because it's possible' there's more.

Firstly, I'll be able to code any widgets or similar interactives in C#, a language which I am fond of compared to JavaScript.

Secondly, I prefer the integrated and unified environment modern .NET provides.

Finally, and most importantly, I can waste some of Microsoft's pennies building my site every time I push to the repo.
