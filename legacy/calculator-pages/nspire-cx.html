History & Background

The Nspire CX line is TI's top-of-the-line series, replacing the Nspire and 89 lines. Notably, unlike Casio's (ClassPads) and HP's (Primes) lines, the Nspire does not have a touchscreen of either type; instead the CX series' D-pad doubles as a trackpad which is used to manipulate a mouse.
